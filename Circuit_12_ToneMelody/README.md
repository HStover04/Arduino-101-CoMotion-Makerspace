# 12 – Tone Melody
The project will use a piezo buzzer/speaker to play a little melody.
## Parts Needed
- (1) Arduino Uno
- (1) USB A-to-B Cable
- (1) Breadboard – Half Size
- (1) Piezo Buzzer/Speaker
- (2) Jumper Wires
